About MuzzleID

We are pioneers in applying advanced AI to solve real-world challenges in agriculture.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower farmers and livestock managers with reliable, non-invasive, and cost-effective technology for animal identification. We believe that by leveraging the power of AI, we can improve animal welfare, enhance food traceability, and increase operational efficiency for farms of all sizes.

Our Technology

MuzzleID is built on a foundation of cutting-edge computer vision models, including Meta's Segment Anything 2 for precise muzzle segmentation and ResNet50 for robust feature extraction. This powerful combination allows us to create a unique digital fingerprint for each animal, ensuring unparalleled accuracy and reliability.

Our Team

We are a passionate team of software engineers, data scientists, and agricultural experts dedicated to revolutionizing the livestock industry. Our diverse expertise allows us to develop innovative solutions that are both technologically advanced and practically grounded.
