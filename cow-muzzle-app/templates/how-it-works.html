How It Works

A simple yet powerful process: from a muzzle image to actionable data in three easy steps.

1

Capture & Upload

Take a clear, high-resolution photo of a cow's muzzle. For best results, ensure the muzzle is clean and well-lit. Upload the image through our secure portal.

2

AI-Powered Analysis

Our system uses the Segment Anything 2 model to precisely isolate the muzzle area. Then, a ResNet50-based feature extractor generates a unique 2048-dimensional "fingerprint" from the muzzle's distinct patterns.

3

Enroll or Identify

When enrolling, the fingerprint is stored with the cow's ID. When identifying, the fingerprint is compared against the entire database using cosine similarity to find the best match with a confidence score above 85%.
